## Using Your Website

### Viewing Your Portfolio

When visitors arrive at your portfolio, they'll experience an immersive introduction with your name appearing through a typing animation effect, followed by your professional tagline. The site features a beautiful dark theme with neon green accents and glassmorphic card designs that create a modern, futuristic aesthetic.

### Exploring Sections

Navigate through your portfolio using the sticky header menu at the top. Click "About" to read your professional introduction, "Skills" to see your technical expertise displayed with interactive progress bars, and "Projects" to view your professional work and academic projects with detailed descriptions and technology stacks.

The "Education" section displays your academic background in a clean timeline format, while the "Contact" section provides multiple ways to reach you.

### Submitting Contact Messages

Visitors can reach out by filling the contact form in the "Get In Touch" section. They'll enter their name, email, and message, then click "Send Message." The form validates all fields and provides instant feedback—successful submissions display a confirmation message, while any errors are clearly indicated.

All contact submissions are securely stored in your database and can be reviewed through the Management UI.

## Managing Your Website

### Dashboard & Analytics

Access the **Dashboard** panel in the Management UI to monitor your portfolio's performance. View visitor analytics, traffic patterns, and engagement metrics to understand how people interact with your content.

### Database Management

Use the **Database** panel to view all contact submissions received through your portfolio's contact form. You can review visitor messages, their contact information, and submission timestamps. Enable SSL in the database connection settings for secure data transmission.

### Customization & Settings

Visit the **Settings** panel to manage your website configuration:

- **General:** Update your website name (VITE_APP_TITLE) and logo (VITE_APP_LOGO) to personalize your portfolio
- **Domains:** Modify your auto-generated domain prefix (xxx.manus.space) or bind a custom domain for a professional web address
- **Secrets:** Manage environment variables and API keys securely

### Code Access

The **Code** panel provides access to all your project files. You can download the entire codebase for backup or local development purposes.
